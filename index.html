Lehigh Valley HACK 2012

This is one amazing weekend bringing together developers to build
exciting and interesting things, learn about new
technology, and share experiences.

You supply the ideas, create teams, and bring a laptop.
We'll supply a great venue, good food, and an incredibly fun time.

Time to build something new in the Valley

With a rich history of manufacturing, the Lehigh Valley also has a unique and growing tech community — and it's time to take the next step.

Developer, hacker, entrepreneur, maker

Whatever title you prefer, join like minded individuals at the first ever event of its kind in the Lehigh Valley area — and help us build something new.
